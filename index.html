ANIMAL
An animal is a living organism that belongs to the kingdom Animalia. Animals are typically characterized by their ability to move, consume organic material they are heterotrophs, and respond to their environment. Most animals have specialized sense organs, nervous systems, and the ability to reproduce sexually (though some reproduce asexually).
